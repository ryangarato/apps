# App Support

Need help with one of our apps? We're here to assist.

## How to Get Support

- **Email**: support@ryan.stream
- **Response Time**: We aim to respond within 2 business days.

## Common Questions

**How do I delete my account or data?**
Email us at support@ryan.stream with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 30 days.

**The app is crashing — what should I do?**
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

**I have a billing issue with an in-app purchase.**
Apple handles all billing. Please visit https://support.apple.com/billing or contact us and we will do our best to assist.

*This support page is maintained to comply with Apple App Store requirements.*
